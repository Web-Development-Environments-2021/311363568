About me


My name is Alter Goldstein, I grew in Polin and survived the holocaust
I immigrated to Israel when I was 20 years old, and built my home at Netanya.
I lived in Netanya my whole life and grow a 3 daughters with my wife Yafa.
My wife die from cancer when she was 60 years old
I was privileged to see 14 grandchildren and the feeling of raising the next generation after experiencing the Holocaust is a feeling of victory for me.
